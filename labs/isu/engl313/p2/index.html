What is Luhn's Algorithm?

Luhn's Algorithm is a method used to validate credit card numbers. It uses a checksum to verify that the card number is valid. Continue reading below to find out how it works!

1) Double every other number starting with the second position from the right. (Move these numbers to a secondary set.)

2) If you get any double-digit numbers, sum the digits together (Ex. 18 = 1 + 8 = 9)

3) Next, sum all of the digits from your secondary set and from the primary set.

4) Divide the total by 10 and you should get a whole number if the card is valid.
