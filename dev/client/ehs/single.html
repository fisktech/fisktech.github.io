Britannica Online School Edition

Rich encyclopedia content plus news headlines, valid Web sites, engaging multimedia, and a customizable workspace.

Gale Student Resources in Context

Designed for use by high school students for both homework and in-depth research assignments. Contains reference works, biographies, essays, overviews, primary works, images, and periodical articles.

World Almanac

Funk & Wagnalls New Encyclopedia and four almanacs. Includes biographies, encyclopedia entries, facts, and statistics. From home, use this Authorization number: 100 376 835 and the universal password on the back of your student ID.

Student Research Center

Provides access to over 4000 professional and peer-review journals, 2000 general magazines, 350 reference books, 80,000 biographies, 80,000 primary source documents, and over 100,000 images, photos and maps.

Gale Directory Library

Hosts a variety of directory products that can be cross-searched for particular companies, organizations or agencies that pertain to your area of interest. Includes names, addresses, email and web sites for each entry.

Gale Group

Collection of 18 multi-subject databases.
