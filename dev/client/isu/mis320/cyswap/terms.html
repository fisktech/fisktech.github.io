SAFETY:

You, as the user, are solely responsible for using this site in a safe manner. Initial contact with another user should be made via email to that users Iowa State University email address. If you agree to buy a product from or sell a product to another user, you should make arrangements to meet in a public place. It is recommended that an exchange of cash (paper currency) be made for any good or service, especially from a person you do not know. A check may bounce. If you are buying or selling an item for a large dollar amount, it is advised that you bring a friend with you to witness the transaction.

Please be advised that when a user clicks on your name on a CySwap post, they will be taken to your entry in the ISU online directory at info.iastate.edu. This directory, by default contains public information about you, including your address. You may want to suppress this information for safety reasons. For example, if you are selling an expensive item, a malicious user could find your address and break in and steal this item while you are away from your residence. You can suppress your address and phone number by clicking on the link that says "How to Suppress My Info" at info.iastate.edu.
