About Us

ServSys Technologies was created with the intention and vision to assist organizations and businesses across the country to use technology more efficiently and to grow, manage, and protect their business.

ServSys is a tech startup providing complete IT solution services based in the middle of the silicon prairie of Des Moines, Iowa, but plans to cover clients nationally and globally. Our company represents a diverse knowledge base of a variety of computer and technology services. We believe in providing every client, big or small, with reliable and secure IT support and services. ServSys Technologies has plans to become a leading IT Services company in the Midwest with a vision to expand globally.

ServSys is dedicated to providing superior solutions precisely tailored to your technological needs. Our staff is comprised of highly skilled professionals in the industry to implement our solutions in order to make sure you receive the best service. ServSys offers a broad selection of computer services that are available as pre-defined package solutions or à la carte customization for special cases. Whatever you need—Computer IT Services, Managed IT Solutions, Network and Domain Services, Data Hosting, Security Auditing, Recovery and Backup, and technological business continuity needs-ServSys will accept nothing less than your complete satisfaction.

ServSys Technologies is committed to work as your Business IT partner through delivering knowledgable, reliable, and high quality standard IT solutions to any organization that requires our services. We understand our client’s needs and demands regardless of the size of their business or their technology issues.

Servicing you to the future of business.
